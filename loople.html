How to Play
✕

Thanks for testing Loople! This is an early build — your feedback helps shape the game. Use the ✉ button to share your thoughts.

Guess the spirograph in 6 tries.

Every spirograph is built from 4 choices:

  • Outer Shape — the frame it draws inside
  • Inner Shape — the piece that rolls around
  • Size — how big the rolling piece is
  • Reach — how far the pen stretches out

Pick one from each group, then tap Roll.

After each guess, you see 4 dots — but not which choice each dot refers to:

In tune — one of your choices is exactly right
Close — one of your choices is one step away
Off — one of your choices is wrong

The catch: you know how many you got right, but not which ones. Study the spirograph and cross-reference your guesses to figure it out. You get 6 tries.

The drawing reveals more with each guess. A new puzzle every day.
