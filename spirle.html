How to Play
✕

Thanks for testing Spirle! This is an early build — your feedback helps shape the game. Use the ✉ button to share your thoughts.

Guess the spirograph in 6 tries.

Every spirograph is built from 4 choices:

  • Outer Shape — the frame it draws inside
  • Inner Shape — the piece that rolls around
  • Size — how big the rolling piece is
  • Reach — how far the pen stretches out

Pick one from each group, then tap Roll.

In tune — you got it right
Close — almost, one step away
Off — not close

The drawing reveals more with each guess. A new puzzle every day.
